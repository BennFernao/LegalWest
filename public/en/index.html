Civil Law

Civil law, also known as private law, is the branch of law that deals with legal relationships between individuals and regulates the rights and duties of individuals in their daily life.

Family Law

Family law covers various aspects of law related to family, such as marriage, divorce, child custody, alimony, adoption, domestic violence, and family planning.

Commercial Law

Commercial law encompasses rules and regulations related to commercial transactions and business activities. It establishes the legal basis for the formation and execution of commercial contracts.

Criminal Law

Criminal law, also known as penal law, is the branch of law that deals with crimes, establishing rules and sanctions for behaviors considered harmful to society.
