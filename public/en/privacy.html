Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal information of users of the Legalwest website. By using our website, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Collected Information

When you access our website, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as name, email address, and phone number. Additionally, we may collect technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device used, pages visited, and activities performed on the website.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Provide and personalize website content and services;
  • Respond to inquiries, questions, and requests;
  • Send important communications, such as updates and relevant information;
  • Improve the user experience on the website;
  • Conduct analysis and research to enhance our services.
3. Sharing of Information

User's personal information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes. However, we may share information with service providers hired to assist with the operation of the website and our activities, provided they are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about website usage, improve its functionality, and provide a personalized experience. Cookies can be disabled in the browser settings, but this may affect the website's functionality.

5. Information Security

We employ appropriate security measures to protect users' personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. This Privacy Policy applies only to our website, so we recommend that you read the privacy policies of any sites visited through those links.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, update, or delete their personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through the available communication channels on the website.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time, with effect from the date of publication on our website. We recommend that you periodically check this page to stay informed about possible changes.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle personal information, please contact us through the available channels on the website.
